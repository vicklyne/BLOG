THOUGHTS

Its well past midnight, thoughts after thoughts haunt my heart

Was it the best solution?

Leting go was not my favorite part of it all

It all comes down to the reason!



Maybe someday the reasons will be clear

Maybe someday I will understand

Maybe someday this will get better

Maybe someday things will change

Maybe just maybe....



Did you see how happy they were?

Did you hear them whisper?

I hoped that you would come to my rescue

I didnt believe you

But I still placed my hope on you
